Frequently asked questions

Which formats does NekoTab support?

British Parliamentary (BP) and other parliamentary styles including Australs/3v3 and WSDC‑style.

Can I import teams, judges, and venues?

Yes — CSV imports are supported, and you can manage availability and conflicts.

Is it free?

NekoTab is free and open‑source. You can self‑host or deploy to providers like Heroku.
