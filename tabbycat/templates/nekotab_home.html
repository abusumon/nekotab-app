Frequently asked questions

Which formats does NekoTab support?

British Parliamentary (BP), Australs/3v3, WSDCâ€‘style, and other parliamentary formats. You can configure speaker counts, sides, and scoring to match your needs.

Yes â€” you can enable public ballot submission or use private URLs sent to participants for secure, individual access.

What is the Motion Doctor?

The Motion Doctor is an AI-powered analysis tool that takes any debate motion and produces a comprehensive coaching-grade report. It includes clash maps, argument suggestions for all teams, Points of Information ideas, extension strategies for BP closing halves, and difficulty/fairness metrics. It uses a 4-stage pipeline (Profiler â†’ Planner â†’ Generator â†’ Validator) to ensure high-quality, motion-specific output.

How does the Motion Bank work?

The Motion Bank is a searchable database of thousands of real debate motions from tournaments worldwide. You can filter by format (BP, Australs, WSDC), region, year, theme, and difficulty level. Each motion includes metadata, community ratings, and the option to run it through the Motion Doctor for AI analysis.

What is the Debate Passport?

The Debate Passport is your personal debate profile that tracks your tournament participation, speaker scores, wins, and achievements across all tournaments hosted on NekoTab. It provides a career overview and connects you with the global debater directory. Think of it as a debate CV that builds automatically as you compete.

Absolutely. NekoTab supports all major school debating formats including WSDC-style and modified Australs.
