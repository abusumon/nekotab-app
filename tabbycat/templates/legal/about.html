Our Mission

NekoTab is a modern debate tournament platform built for the global parliamentary debating community. We provide free, open-source tabulation software alongside AI-powered debate tools — making it easier for organisers to run tournaments and for debaters to improve their skills.

What We Offer

  • Tournament Tabulation — Full-featured tab software supporting British Parliamentary (BP), Australs, WSDC, and other parliamentary formats. Manage draws, allocations, ballots, standings, and break categories.
  • Motion Doctor — AI-powered motion analysis providing clash maps, POI suggestions, team strategies, extensions, and fairness metrics for any debate motion.
  • Global Motion Bank — A searchable database of thousands of real tournament motions, filterable by format, region, year, and theme.
  • Debate Forum — A structured discussion space for debaters to share strategies, analyze motions, and learn from the community.
  • Debate Passport — Career profiles tracking tournament participation, performance, and achievements across the global circuit.

Built on Open Source

NekoTab is built on the foundation of Tabbycat, the leading open-source debate tabulation system developed by volunteers. We extend this foundation with additional features for the modern debate community while maintaining our commitment to open-source principles.

Who We Are

NekoTab is maintained by a small team of passionate debaters and developers who believe in making debate technology accessible to everyone — from university societies to national circuits and international championships.
